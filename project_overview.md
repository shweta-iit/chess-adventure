# Chess Adventures

## Vision
A playful chess learning experience for kids ages 6–8.

## MVP Goal
Help kids:
1. Learn a chess piece
2. Solve simple puzzles
3. Play a beginner game with an AI or computer opponent.

## Core Product Principles
- Simple over feature-rich
- Encouraging over competitive
- Reusable systems over custom screens

## Initial Scope
- Homepage
- Learn section
- Puzzle section
- Play section
- Rewards

## Non Goals
- Multiplayer
- Accounts
- Complex AI coaching
- Advanced animations
